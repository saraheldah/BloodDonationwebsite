Requirements by Donation Type



To ensure the safety of both patients and donors, these are some of
the requirements donors must meet to be eligible to donate blood
based on their donation type.


Whole Blood Donation
  1. Donation frequency: Every 56 days.
  2. You must be in good health and feeling well.
  3. You must be at least 16 years old.
  4. You must weigh at least 50 Kgs.

Power Red Donation
  1. Donation frequency: Every 112 days, up to 3 times/year.
  2. You must be in good health and feeling well.
  3. Male donors must be at least 17 years old, at least l53 cm tall and weigh at least 59 Kgs.
  4. Female donors must be at least 19 years old, at least l53 cm tall and weigh at least 68 Kgs.

Platelet Donation
  1. Donation frequency: Every 7 days, up to 24 times/year.
  2. You must be in good health and feeling well.
  3. You must be at least 17 years old.
  4. You must weigh at least 50 Kgs.

AB Elite Plasma Donation
  1. Donation frequency: Every 28 days, up to 13 times/year.
  2. You must have type AB blood.
  3. You must be in good health and feeling well.
  4. You must be at least 17 years old.
  5. You must weigh at least 50 Kgs.
